Profil toko

Toko kami berdiri sejak tahun 2030 dan memiliki mitra-mitra petani terprercaya dengan produk berkualitas tanpa pestisida.

Toko kami berada di jl.Kenangan Terindah No.1, Kabupaten Khayalan. Toko kami berdiri setinggi tiga lantai dengan luas 750m2.Tempat parkir kami dapat menampung 100 kendaraan roda empat dan 300 kendaraan roda dua.

Our shop was founded in 2030 and has trusted farmer partners with quality products without pesticides.

Our shop is located on Jl. Kenangan Terindah No.1, Khayan District. Our shop stands three floors high with an area of ​​750m2. Our parking lot can accommodate 100 four-wheeled vehicles and 300 two-wheeled vehicles.
